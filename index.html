Animal Based Diet - Guidelines:
Low PUFA, Low Oxalate, Low Linoleic Acid

The Animal-Based Diet, popularized by Dr. Paul Saladino, emphasizes nutrient-dense animal foods along with fruit, honey, and dairy. It limits seed oils, high-PUFA fats, and certain plant foods to help reduce inflammation and support overall metabolic health.

EAT

Focus: Ruminant meat + organs as foundation, low-oxalate/low-PUFA fruits, honey, raw/full-fat dairy (if tolerated). Minimize oxalates, linoleic acid (PUFA), plant toxins.
